------------------------
Tractor Beam [(blue)]
------------------------

Basic mode:
	Move a target 0, 1 or 2 squares to a square you can see, and give it 1 damage

Secondary mode 1 - Punisher Mode:
	Choose a target 0, 1 or 2 moves away form you. Move the target to your square and deal 3 damage to it. [red, yellow]

Notes:
	You can move a target even if you can't see it- The target ends up in a place where you can see and damage it. The moves do not have to be in the same direction.
