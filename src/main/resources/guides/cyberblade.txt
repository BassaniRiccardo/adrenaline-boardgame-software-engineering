------------------------
Cyberblade [(yellow), red]
------------------------

Basic effect:
	Deal 2 damage to 1 target on your square.

Optional effect 1 - Shadowstep:
	Move 1 square before or after the basic effect.

Optional effect 1 - Slice and Dice:
	Deal 2 damage to a different target on your square. The shadowstep may be used before or after this effect.

	Notes: Combining all effects allows you to move onto a square and whack 2 people; or whack somebody, move, and whack somebody else; or whack 2 people and then move.
