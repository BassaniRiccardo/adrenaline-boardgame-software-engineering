------------------------
Plasma Gun [(blue), yellow]
------------------------

Basic effect:
	Deal 2 damage to 1 target you can see.

Optional effect 1 - Phase Glide:
	Move 1 or 2 squares. This effect can be used either before or after the basic effect.

Optional effect 2 - Charged Shot:
	Deal 1 additional damage to your target. [blue]

Notes:
	The two moves have no ammo cost. You don't have to ba able to see your target whan you play the card. For example, you can move 2 squares and shoot a target you now see. You cannot use 1 move before shooting and 1 move after.
