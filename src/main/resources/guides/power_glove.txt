------------------------
Power Glove [(yellow), blue]
------------------------

Basic effect:
	Choose 1 target on any square exactly 1 move away. Move onto that square and give the target 1 damage and 2 marks.

Secondary effect - Rocket Fist:
	Choose a square exactly 1 move away. Move onto that square. You may deal 2 damage to 1 target there. If you want, you may move 1 more square in that same direction (but only if it is a legal move). You may deal 2 damage to 1 target there, as well. [blue]

	Notes: In rocket fist mode, you're flying 2 squares in a straight line, punching 1 person per square.
